Skilled — Sponsored visa (subclass 176)The Skilled-Sponsored visa (subclass 176) is closed to new applications. This visa was subject to the Cap and Cease arrangement that took effect on 22 September 2015. Any visa application that was not decided before 22 September 2015 will be taken not to have been made.  For more information on the cap and cease arrangement go to Cap and cease.

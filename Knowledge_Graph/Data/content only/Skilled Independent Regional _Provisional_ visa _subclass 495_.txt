Skilled — Independent Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 495)The Skilled - Independent Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 495) closed to new applications on 1 September 2007. You might be able to get the Skilled – Regional (Provisional) (subclass 489) visa.
Members of your family unit might be able to get the Skilled – Regional (Provisional) (subclass 489) visa if you already hold this visa and they want to join you in Australia.

Medical Treatment (Short Stay) visa (subclass 675)The Medical Treatment (Short Stay) visa (subclass 675) closed to new applications on 23 March 2013. You might be able to get the Medical Treatment visa (subclass 602) visa if you want to come in to Australia for medical treatment or consultations.
If you have been granted the Medical Treatment (Short Stay) visa (subclass 675), it will be valid until the expiry date you were given when your visa was granted

Electronic Travel Authority (Visitor) (subclass 976)You can no longer apply for an Electronic Travel Authority (Visitor) (subclass 976). It closed to new applications on 22 March 2013. You might be able to apply for the Electronic Travel Authority (subclass 601).
1. Electronic travel authority holders
This information is for people who have already been granted an Electronic Travel Authority (Visitor) (subclass 976). It explains your rights and obligations.
You can use the free Visa Entitlement Verification Online (VEVO) to check your visa status and entitlements.

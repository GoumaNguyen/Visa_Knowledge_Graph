2. Sponsors
This information is for approved sponsors of people on a Sport visa (subclass 421). You cannot apply to sponsor applicants for this visa because it is no longer open to new applications.
When your sponsorship approval ends, if you want to sponsor anyone else as a sports person you will need to search the visa finder tool for an appropriate visa.
You can use  Visa Entitlement Verification Online (VEVO for Organisations) to check the visa details and entitlements of a person that you have sponsored.
Your sponsorship is valid for up to three years.
If you want to sponsor someone as a sports person after that time, you will need to search the visa finder tool for an appropriate visa.
